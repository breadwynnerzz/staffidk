# Community Interaction

Staff members are expected to help create a positive, active, and welcoming community environment. This is especially important during early development when community growth and retention are major priorities.

## Community Responsibilities

| Responsibility | Expected Behavior |
| --- | --- |
| Welcoming members | Greet new players and help them understand the community. |
| Answering questions | Provide accurate information or direct players to the right place. |
| Encouraging activity | Start conversations and support healthy engagement. |
| Representing staff | Stay mature and avoid drama, toxicity, or favoritism. |
| Supporting retention | Help players feel heard, respected, and guided. |

## Staff Presence

Staff should be visible in a positive way. A strong staff presence does not mean controlling every conversation. It means being helpful, consistent, and approachable.

> [!NOTE]
> During the early stage of the server, community involvement is a major part of staff activity.
