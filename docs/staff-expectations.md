# Staff Expectations

Staff must stay active, respectful, fair, and professional while representing Brick City RP. Decisions should be based on rules, evidence, and context, not personal feelings.

> [!DANGER]
> Low activity, favoritism, abuse of permissions, or repeated poor conduct may lead to discipline or removal.

## Core Standards

| Expectation | Standard |
| --- | --- |
| Activity | Contribute to Discord, tickets, reports, or staff tasks. |
| Professionalism | Stay mature in public and staff spaces. |
| Fairness | Avoid bias and personal conflicts. |
| Rule Knowledge | Understand and enforce server rules correctly. |
| Communication | Be clear, respectful, and accurate. |

## Staff Must Not

- Abuse staff tools
- Protect friends from punishment
- Argue with players
- Leak staff information
- Ignore assigned responsibilities
- Punish without proof
