# Staff Hierarchy

Staff must follow the chain of command. Do not skip ranks unless the situation is urgent or your direct lead is unavailable.

```text
Owner
Developer
Head Honcho
Board of Directors
Head Administration
Administrator
Head Moderator
Moderator
Trial Moderator
Support
Applications Team
```

> [!WARNING]
> Bypassing leadership can create confusion, duplicate decisions, and staff conflict.

## Basic Role Flow

| Level | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Ownership | Final decisions and server direction. |
| Development | Server systems, scripts, and stability. |
| Administration | Escalated reports and major punishments. |
| Moderation | Player reports and rule enforcement. |
| Support | Tickets, questions, and basic assistance. |

Escalate anything severe, unclear, or outside your authority.
