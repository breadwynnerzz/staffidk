# Administrator Responsibilities

Administrators oversee moderation, support lower staff, and handle escalated situations fairly.

## Main Duties

| Duty | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Escalated Reports | Review evidence and policy. |
| Staff Support | Help lower staff follow procedure. |
| Major Punishments | Confirm evidence before approval. |
| Internal Issues | Help resolve staff concerns. |
| Standards | Lead by example. |

## Administrator Standard

Administrators must be calm, consistent, active, and professional. They should not create conflict or issue unfair punishments.

> [!DANGER]
> Authority abuse, favoritism, or repeated poor handling can lead to demotion or removal.
