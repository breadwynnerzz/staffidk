# Abuse of Permissions

Staff permissions exist only for assigned duties. They may not be used for personal gain, favoritism, retaliation, intimidation, or entertainment.

## Not Allowed

| Abuse | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Favoritism | Protecting friends from punishment. |
| Targeting | Punishing someone due to personal dislike. |
| Personal Gain | Using tools to help yourself or your group. |
| Unapproved Action | Issuing major punishment without approval. |
| Leaks | Sharing logs, tickets, or staff discussions. |

## Conflict of Interest

If a situation involves a friend, group, or personal issue, step back and let neutral staff handle it when possible.

> [!DANGER]
> Bias, favoritism, or authority abuse can lead to demotion or removal.
