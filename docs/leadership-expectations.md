# Leadership Expectations

Leadership staff set the example for the entire team. They must be consistent, calm, fair, and willing to correct issues before they grow.

## Leadership Standards

| Standard | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Consistency | Apply policy evenly. |
| Oversight | Guide lower staff when needed. |
| Documentation | Log serious actions clearly. |
| Accountability | Accept feedback and correct mistakes. |
| Privacy | Handle staff issues privately when possible. |

## Role Focus

- Ownership and management handle direction, staffing, and major decisions.
- Administration handles escalated reports and major moderation issues.
- Moderation leadership supports moderators and keeps enforcement consistent.
- Training leadership handles onboarding and evaluations.

> [!NOTE]
> Leadership should correct behavior professionally, not embarrass staff publicly.
