# Inactivity Policies

Repeated inactivity, lack of contribution, avoiding responsibilities, or consistently failing to assist the community may result in warnings, demotion, or termination from the staff team.

## Inactivity Examples

| Behavior | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Sitting online without helping | Does not contribute to staff responsibilities. |
| Ignoring tickets or reports | Leaves players unsupported. |
| Avoiding staff duties | Creates extra work for active staff. |
| No communication | Prevents management from planning coverage. |
| Repeated absence | Reduces trust in the staff member's reliability. |

## Leave of Absence

If you need time away, contact ownership or management before becoming inactive. Include:

- Expected start date
- Expected return date
- Short reason if comfortable sharing
- Any responsibilities that need coverage

> [!WARNING]
> Failure to communicate inactivity may negatively impact your staff position.
