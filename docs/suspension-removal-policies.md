# Suspension & Removal Policies

Staff roles are earned privileges. Management may warn, suspend, demote, or remove staff if expectations are not met.

## Promotions

Promotions are based on trust, activity, maturity, professionalism, leadership, and contribution. They are not based on friendships or time in rank.

> [!WARNING]
> Do not ask, pressure, or complain publicly about promotions.

## Discipline Reasons

| Reason | Possible Result |
| --- | --- |
| Inactivity | Warning, demotion, or removal. |
| Permission abuse | Suspension or removal. |
| Favoritism | Discipline or demotion. |
| Toxic behavior | Suspension or removal. |
| Leaking staff info | Immediate removal. |
| Poor handling | Retraining, warning, or demotion. |

Management may review staff performance at any time.
