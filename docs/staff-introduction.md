# Staff Introduction

Welcome to the **Brick City RP Staff Team**. This handbook is for internal staff use only. It explains expectations, procedures, punishments, communication standards, and leadership structure.

Staff are expected to stay mature, fair, active, and professional while representing the community.

> [!WARNING]
> Staff positions are privileges. Abuse, favoritism, toxicity, or poor conduct can lead to discipline or removal.

## Current Stage

Brick City RP is still developing. Staff are not expected to handle full in-game moderation unless management says otherwise.

During this stage, activity means:

- Engaging in Discord
- Helping players with questions
- Supporting community growth
- Promoting positive activity
- Representing Brick City RP well

| Principle | Standard |
| --- | --- |
| Fairness | Treat players consistently. |
| Professionalism | Stay calm and respectful. |
| Accountability | Own mistakes and improve. |
| Chain of Command | Escalate properly. |
