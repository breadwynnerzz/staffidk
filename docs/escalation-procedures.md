# Escalation Procedures

Escalate anything severe, unclear, personally connected to you, or outside your authority.

## When To Escalate

| Situation | Send To |
| --- | --- |
| Major punishment | Administrator or higher. |
| Staff misconduct | Management or ownership. |
| Friend or group involved | Neutral staff. |
| Rule confusion | Head Moderator or higher. |
| Server-wide issue | Management or developer. |

## Escalation Format

```text
Situation:
Players involved:
Evidence:
Rule involved:
Action taken:
Review needed:
```

> [!DANGER]
> Failing to escalate properly can lead to staff discipline.
