# Professionalism Standards

Staff must stay mature, respectful, and calm at all times. Personal feelings should never affect staff decisions.

## Expected Behavior

- Speak respectfully
- Avoid drama and arguments
- Keep staff information private
- Handle players fairly
- Stay calm during conflict
- Ask for help when unsure

## Not Allowed

| Behavior | Issue |
| --- | --- |
| Arguing | Escalates situations. |
| Toxicity | Damages community trust. |
| Favoritism | Breaks fairness. |
| Public staff drama | Weakens the team. |
| Emotional punishment | Leads to poor decisions. |

> [!DANGER]
> Poor professionalism can lead to warnings, demotion, suspension, or removal.
