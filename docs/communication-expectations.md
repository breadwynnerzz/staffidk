# Communication Expectations

Staff communication must be clear, respectful, and accurate in Discord, tickets, reports, and staff spaces.

## Standards

| Situation | Standard |
| --- | --- |
| Questions | Give clear, correct answers. |
| Tickets | Stay patient and focused. |
| Reports | Ask for evidence and context. |
| Staff Chat | Keep it respectful and private. |
| Escalations | Summarize facts and needed action. |

## Avoid

- Public arguments
- Insults or mocking
- Guessing policy
- Sharing staff disputes
- Speaking for management without approval

> [!NOTE]
> If unsure, ask higher staff before answering.
