# Ticket Handling Procedures

Tickets must be handled calmly, professionally, and without bias. Staff should focus on solving the issue, not arguing.

## Ticket Flow

1. Read the full ticket.
2. Ask for missing details or evidence.
3. Review rules, logs, and context.
4. Resolve or escalate.
5. Summarize the outcome.
6. Close the ticket if complete.

## Standards

| Standard | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Patience | Do not rush players. |
| Accuracy | Do not guess. |
| Privacy | Keep staff details internal. |
| Escalation | Send serious issues upward. |

```text
Please provide your name, issue, involved players, and any evidence.
```

> [!DANGER]
> Careless ticket handling or false punishment can lead to staff discipline.
